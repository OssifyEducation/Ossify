Common questions.

What's included in every plan?
Every plan includes full access to the complete Ossify question bank — all 1,200+ questions across both papers, all 4 revision modes, your personal performance dashboard, and all clinical explanations. There are no tiered features.
Can I switch plans or upgrade?
Yes. If you purchase a shorter plan and wish to extend, contact us and we'll apply the difference towards your upgrade.
Which plan should I choose?
Most candidates benefit most from the 6-month plan. It gives you enough time to work through the question bank systematically, revisit weak areas using spaced repetition, and run multiple timed mocks before exam day.
Why is there no free trial?
Every question on Ossify is individually written by practising doctors, peer-reviewed for clinical accuracy, and refined through multiple rounds of editorial checks. That level of quality takes significant time and expertise to produce. Offering questions freely — even a subset — would mean distributing content that represents hundreds of hours of clinical work, and it would attract users who are not genuinely committed to their preparation. We believe in a straightforward arrangement: you pay for access, and in return you get a question bank that has been built to the highest possible standard. That standard is the product. It doesn't need a free sample to justify itself.
