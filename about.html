About Ossify

Built by Doctors.
Designed for success.

Our mission is simple: to give every doctor the best possible chance of securing the specialty they've worked for — through smarter preparation, better questions, and a deeper understanding of what the MSRA actually demands.

Ossify was created by a group of practising doctors who experienced firsthand what it means to prepare for the MSRA with inadequate tools. The question banks available on the market felt recycled, the explanations were shallow, and the learning — well, it barely felt like learning at all.

We believe that preparing for specialty training should make you a better clinician, not just a better guesser. So we built Ossify: a question bank platform rooted in genuine clinical reasoning, written and reviewed by doctors who understand exactly what the MSRA tests and why.

Every question on Ossify is crafted to reflect the real complexity of the Professional Dilemmas and Clinical Problem Solving papers. We don't pad our question bank with filler. We obsess over quality — the accuracy of the clinical content, the fairness of the distractors, the depth of the explanations, and the relevance to how you'll actually practise medicine.

We are a team of doctors who sat where you're sitting. That shapes everything about how this platform is built.

What we believe.

01
Quality over volume
We don't pad our question bank with filler. Every question earns its place — written, reviewed, and refined for clinical accuracy and educational value.
02
Understanding over memorisation
The MSRA rewards clinical reasoning, not rote recall. Our questions and explanations are designed to build understanding that transfers to new scenarios.
03
Better preparation, better doctors
Preparing well for the MSRA shouldn't mean becoming a question-answering machine. Ossify is built to make you a better clinician in the process.

Ossify was built out of frustration — and out of genuine care for our colleagues navigating one of the most consequential exams of their careers.
