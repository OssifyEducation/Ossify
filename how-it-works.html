How it works

Smart preparation,
built around you.

Everything on Ossify is designed to maximise learning — not just practice time. A structured revision system that adapts to your performance, identifies your weak spots, and helps you build deep, retrievable knowledge that holds up under exam pressure.

01
Step one
Practise the way you learn best
Ossify gives you full control over how you revise. Whether you are working through a targeted topic, simulating full exam conditions, or reviewing material you have already covered, there is a mode built for it.
Timed exam mode
Simulate the real MSRA under exam conditions. Fixed time limits, randomised questions, and a full performance breakdown at the end.
Tutor mode
Answer questions at your own pace with immediate, detailed explanations after each one. Ideal for active learning and understanding the reasoning.
Topic mode
Focus on specific clinical areas. Build confidence in your weak areas systematically before moving on to the next topic.
Revision mode
Revisit questions you have flagged, answered incorrectly, or marked for review. Spaced repetition built in.
02
Step two
Questions that actually teach you something
Every Ossify question is written by practising doctors and reviewed for clinical accuracy, exam relevance, and educational value. Our explanations go beyond telling you the correct answer.
  • Why the correct answer is right — the clinical reasoning, the guideline, the principle behind it.
  • Why the distractors are wrong — addressing the exact misconceptions that trip candidates up.
  • What to remember for similar questions — consolidating the learning so it transfers to new scenarios.
